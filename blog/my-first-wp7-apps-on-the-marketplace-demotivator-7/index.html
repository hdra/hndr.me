My First WP7 Apps on the Marketplace–Demotivator 7

Written on Jan 10, 2012

Ok, so I mentioned that I am developing several Windows Phone 7 Apps, and I actually got one of them published on the marketplace! Most of the others are still unpublished yet though, either its going on review, or well, I just didn’t finish it.

So, here is the one that got it to the marketplace. Demotivator 7, it’s a application to create demotivator posters from the pictures stored in the phone. The download link is here.

There are some bugs when creating a portrait poster, I already fixed that, but I just don’t feel like updating it yet because of the troublesome upload process, so, maybe later.
